Roof Washing

Roof cleaning is the process of removing algae, Mold, mildew, lichen, and moss from roofs. Also cleaning oxidation on metal roofs. Cleaning can extend the duration of a roof’s ability to function. Algae and other types of build-ups often form on the north and west parts of roofs that are shaded or receive less sun and can reduce a roof’s life expectancy.

Frequently wash your roof to prolong the life expectancy of your roof.

Roofing Asset Management Plan (RAMP)

We assist building owners and facility managers to effectively take ownership of the roof life cycle. As part of regular building maintenance, roof inspection and maintenance are critical to an overall preventive maintenance program of a building.

Planned and scheduled roof maintenance can help ensure that all roofs will provide satisfactory performance for their full life expectancy, and help companies reduce drastically the cost of their annual maintenance budget.
